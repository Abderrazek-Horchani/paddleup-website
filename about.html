About PaddleUp

Welcome to PaddleUp, your premier destination for water sports adventures in Tunisia!

Our Story

Founded in 2023, PaddleUp was born from a passion for water sports and a desire to share the beauty of Tunisia's coastline with both locals and visitors. What started as a small operation with just one kayak has grown into a thriving business offering a range of water sports equipment and experiences.

Our Mission

At PaddleUp, we're committed to providing safe, enjoyable, and memorable water sports experiences. We believe in:

  • Promoting water sports as a healthy, fun activity
  • Ensuring the safety of all our customers
  • Protecting and preserving our marine environment
  • Providing excellent customer service

Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. We provide:

  • Life jackets for all customers
  • Basic safety instructions
  • Emergency contact information
  • Designated safe areas for water sports
